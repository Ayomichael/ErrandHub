ErrandHub

ErrandHub is a convenient service application that enables you to outsource and effectively manage your daily tasks, chores, errands, and repairs. The app provides a platform to organize your to-do list, allowing you to focus on more important tasks. The app also provides estimated time(Time Management) frames for completing each task, ensuring that they are carried out swiftly. The app can help you avoid the stress that comes with going to the market, So You can have meals delivered from your kitchen to your dining table, without worrying about bargaining, traffic, or bad weather conditions. This app provides a platform for minor repairs and fixes: Artisans are available on the app, and they have been vetted, ensuring that the right person is assigned to the job. Reviews and ratings of the artisans' work are also available on the app. The app is suitable for both young and old people and can benefit anyone who wants to simplify their life in a city like Lagos.
